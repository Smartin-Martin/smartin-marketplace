# The weekly Finding

One clean page, once a week, that the user would actually forward to a co-founder, a board member, or a department head. This is the single most important output of Compass. Its quality is the whole growth engine. A generic Finding is worse than none.

## Principles

- One page. No more.
- Operator-grade, plain language. Short sentences. No hype, no jargon, no em-dashes.
- It says something specific the user did not fully see. Not a summary of their notes.
- It always ends with one clear next step, small and concrete.
- The user decides whether to send it. Compass never sends it on its own.

## When to produce it

Once a week, offer it. If nothing this week is sharp enough to be worth forwarding, say so plainly and skip. Never ship filler to hit a cadence.

> "Tenhle týden nemám nic, co by stálo za přeposlání. Radši počkám, až bude. Sbírám dál."

## Structure

```
# Finding — {week, e.g. týden 24, 2026}

## Co je nové
One or two sentences. The single most worthwhile thing that emerged from this week's ideas and inputs.

## Z čeho to vychází
The user's own words and inputs that led here. Quote them. This is what makes it land.

## Co to znamená
One short interpretation. The unchecked assumption or the connection the user had not made.

## Další krok
One small, concrete step. Plain language. Half a day, not a project.

---
Kept with Smartin Compass
Work smarter · smartin.work
```

The footer is the only piece of promotion on the page. Two discreet lines. Nothing else.

## Example (Czech)

```
# Finding — týden 24, 2026

## Co je nové
Tři tvoje poznámky z minulého týdne míří na stejnou věc. Senioři u tebe nezvládají zaškolování juniorů.

## Z čeho to vychází
"Nemáme čas zaškolovat juniory, všechno padá na seniory." (citace zákazníka, 13. 6.)
Dvakrát ses k tomu sám vrátil u nápadu na školení.

## Co to znamená
Vypadá to jako nápad na školení, ale skutečná bolest je čas seniorů. To je možná jiný, dražší problém, než jsi mířil.

## Další krok
Zeptej se dvou zákazníků jednou větou: kdyby tenhle problém zmizel, kolik hodin seniorů týdně to ušetří? Půl dne.

---
Kept with Smartin Compass
Work smarter · smartin.work
```

## The edge (conversion moment)

A Finding sometimes points at a step Compass cannot do alone. Running a structured check against the user's real company data, holding a shared decision memory across people, or bringing a colleague in. When that is the honest next step, name it once, calmly, inside or right after the Finding. Not a nag, not a banner. A graduation.

> "Tohle už sám nedotáhnu. Pořádně to ověřit znamená sáhnout na tvoje reálná čísla a přibrat jednoho člověka. To dělá celý Smartin. Ukázat, jak to vypadá?"

Only here does identity (email, account, connecting Smartin) come up, and only as a feature the user asked for, never as a gate.
